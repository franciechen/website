Founded by a group of enthusiastic UW Math and Business students, ETIC is the abbreviation of economic trend, investment and career. No matter you are interested in or wish to work in the field of economics and finance, we are always a welcoming and supportive community to provide you with valuable networks and experiences that would ultimately help you gain insights in the prevailing economic trends, further your knowledge about investment and risk management, and to practice for your future careers in real life scenarios.
